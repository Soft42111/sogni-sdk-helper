# 🌌 The Sogni Manifesto: The Decentralized AI Revolution

Welcome to the definitive technical manual for the **Sogni SDK**. This document serves as the conceptual foundation for understanding not just a library, but a paradigm shift in how artificial intelligence is computed, distributed, and consumed.

## 🏛️ The Philosophy of DePIN

At its core, Sogni is built upon the principle of **DePIN** (Decentralized Physical Infrastructure Network). Traditional AI is siloed within the massive data centers of "Big Tech." These centralized entities control the models, the data, and the hardware, creating single points of failure and potential for censorship.

Sogni breaks this monopoly by leveraging the **Sogni Supernet**.

### The Sogni Supernet
The Supernet is a "network of networks"—a global, decentralized collective of GPU nodes provided by individuals and specialized data centers. By distributing inference tasks across this network, Sogni achieves:
- **Censorship Resilience**: No single entity can shut down the network.
- **Cost Efficiency**: Competitive pricing driven by a decentralized marketplace.
- **Performance at Scale**: Access to massive, elastic compute power on demand.

## 🏗️ Architectural Hierarchy

To master the Sogni SDK, one must understand the three tiers of the ecosystem:

### 1. The Protocol Layer (The Supernet)
The decentralized orchestrator that manages job distribution, worker health, and result verification. It ensures that when you request a "Cyberpunk Cityscape," the most suitable worker is assigned the task based on your chosen network speed.

### 2. The SDK Layer (`@sogni-ai/sogni-client`)
This is your primary interface. It acts as a sophisticated bridge that handles:
- **WebSocket State Management**: Persistent, real-time communication.
- **Reactive Entities**: Managing the lifecycle of `Project` and `Job` instances.
- **Authentication**: Seamlessly handling API keys and wallet-linked credentials.

### 3. The Execution Layer (Workers)
The actual hardware performing the math. Sogni categorizes these into two distinct networks:
- ⚡ **Fast Network**: Powered by high-end NVIDIA GPUs (RTX 3090/4090, A100/H100). Essential for high-resolution video and rapid image synthesis.
- 🍏 **Relaxed Network**: Primarily powered by Apple Silicon (Mac devices). Optimized for cost-effective image generation where speed is less critical than price.
