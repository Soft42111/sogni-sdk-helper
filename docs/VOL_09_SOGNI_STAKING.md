# 📈 Vol. 09: $SOGNI Staking & Worker Rewards

At the heart of the Sogni DePIN governance is the **$SOGNI** native token. This volume explores how $SOGNI incentivizes high-performance workers and allows the community to participate in the network's growth through staking.

---

## 🏛️ The Role of $SOGNI

$SOGNI is the cryptographic representation of value within the Supernet. It serves three primary functions:
1.  **Incentivization**: Rewarding hardware providers (Workers) for successful AI inference.
2.  **Staking**: Securing the network and earning a percentage of platform fees.
3.  **Governance**: Proposing and voting on protocol changes (e.g., adding a new model family).

---

## 👷 Worker Rewards Logic

When a worker joins the Supernet, their earnings are calculated based on several performance metrics.

### 1. Proof of Completion
Every successful job earns a base reward in $SOGNI. This reward is proportional to the compute complexity of the task (Image vs. Video).

### 2. Multipliers
- **Uptime Multiplier**: Workers with 99.9% uptime earn more per task.
- **Latency Multiplier**: Fast responders (low RTT to users) are prioritized for high-value jobs.
- **Accuracy Multiplier**: Nodes that consistently pass Proof of Inference audits receive a "Karma" bonus.

---

## 🥩 The Staking Protocol

Sogni allows anyone to support the network by "Staking" their $SOGNI tokens. Staking involves "locking" your tokens in a smart contract for a set period.

### Benefits of Staking:
- **Reward Dividends**: Stakers earn a portion of the Spark-to-SOGNI conversion fees generated by the platform.
- **Network Influence**: Your voting power in the Sogni DAO is proportional to your staked amount.
- **Priority Access**: High-tier stakers may receive early access to experimental "Beta" models.

### How to Stake via the SDK:
While most staking is done via the Sogni Dashboard, the SDK provides balance-checking for staked assets.
```javascript
const user = await sogni.account.me();
console.log('Liquid SOGNI:', user.balance.sogni);
console.log('Staked SOGNI:', user.balance.stakedSogni);
```

---

## 🔒 Slashing & Security

To protect the network from malicious workers, Sogni implements **Slashing**. 
- If a worker node is caught submitting fake data or intentionally harming the user's experience (e.g., repeating failed jobs), a portion of their *security deposit* (staked $SOGNI) may be slashed (burned).

---

## 📉 Token Circulation

The Supernet protocol manages the supply of $SOGNI dynamically.
- **Minting**: New tokens are minted to reward workers.
- **Burning**: A portion of every transaction fee is "burned" (sent to a null address), creating a deflationary pressure as the network's usage increases.
