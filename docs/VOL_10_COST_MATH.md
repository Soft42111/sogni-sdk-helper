# 📐 Vol. 10: Cost Estimations: The Mathematics of Billing

Generating high-fidelity AI media across a decentralized network requires a predictable and fair pricing model. This volume breaks down the specific mathematical formulas used by the Sogni Supernet to calculate Spark and SOGNI costs for every modality.

---

## 🏛️ The Variable Cost Matrix

Costs on the Supernet are not "fixed" per request. They are derived from the total computational load placed on the worker nodes.

### 1. Image Generation Formula
The primary drivers of image cost are **Resolution** and **Step Count**.

**`Cost_Image = (Resolution_Factor * Step_Factor * Model_Weight)`**

- **Resolution_Factor**: Derived from the total pixel count. 512x512 is the base (1.0). 1024x1024 is 4.0.
- **Step_Factor**: Every denoising pass costs compute. 20 steps is 1.0. 40 steps is 2.0.
- **Model_Weight**: Premium models like **Flux** have a higher multiplier (e.g., 2.5x) than legacy models like **SD 1.5** (1.0x).

---

## 📽️ Video Generation Complexity

Video is significantly more expensive than image generation because it requires temporal self-attention across multiple frames.

### WAN 2.2 Pricing
Since WAN uses a fixed internal FPS (16), the cost is strictly tied to duration.
**`Cost_WAN = (Duration_Seconds * Resolution_Factor * 1.5)`**

### LTX-2.3 Pricing
Since LTX generates actual frames at your specified FPS, the cost scales with the frame count.
**`Cost_LTX = (Total_Frames * Resolution_Factor * 1.2)`**
*(Recall: Frames = Duration * FPS + 1)*

## 🤖 LLM & Cognitive Costs

LLM usage is billed by the **Token**.
- **Input Tokens**: The prompt and context history.
- **Output Tokens**: The response generated by the model.
- **Thinking Mode**: Reasoning tokens (`<think>` blocks) ARE billed at the same rate as output tokens.

**`Cost_LLM = (Input_Tokens * Rate_In) + (Output_Tokens * Rate_Out)`**

## 🛠️ Programmatic Estimation in the SDK

The SDK provides an `estimateCost` utility to help you build "Checkout" or "Credit Check" flows in your UI.

```javascript
const estimate = await sogni.projects.estimateCost({
  type: 'video',
  modelId: 'ltx23-22b-fp8_t2v_dev',
  duration: 8,
  fps: 24
});

console.log('Estimated Spark:', estimate.spark);
console.log('Estimated SOGNI:', estimate.sogni);
```

## 📈 Price Fluctuations

In the decentralized marketplace, worker rewards are occasionally adjusted based on **Network Load** and **Energy Costs**.
- **Congestion Multiplier**: During peak hours, the base cost may increase slightly to incentivize more workers to join the network.
- **Model Deprecation**: As hardware becomes more efficient, legacy models may see their `Model_Weight` reduced over time.
